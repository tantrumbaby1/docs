Configure how OneClick interacts with the Solana blockchain and various transaction services. Optimize for speed, cost, and reliability based on your trading style.

<Card icon="exchange" title="Transaction Configuration">

</Card>

## Priority Fee Management

### Fee Strategy

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Conservative"
    icon="shield"
  >
    **Cost-optimized approach**:
    - Lower priority fees
    - Accepts longer confirmation times
    - Suitable for non-urgent trades
    - Maximizes profit margins
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Aggressive"
    icon="zap"
  >
    **Speed-optimized approach**:
    - Higher priority fees
    - Fastest possible confirmations
    - Critical for time-sensitive trades
    - Reduced profit margins
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Fee Calculation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Base Fee">
    **Network base fee**: Current Solana network base fee (typically very low)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Priority Fee">
    **Your priority addition**: Additional fee to prioritize your transaction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service Fee">
    **Service provider fee**: Cost for using premium transaction services
  </Step>
  <Step title="Total Cost">
    **Final transaction cost**: Sum of all fees for complete transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>
