# Advanced Settings

Configure OneClick's most powerful automation features including farmer mode, batch deployment, advanced algorithms, and professional trading tools.

<Card icon="cog" title="Advanced Configuration">
Access advanced settings through **Settings > Advanced** to enable automation features, configure batch operations, and access professional trading tools.
</Card>

<Warning>
**Advanced Features**: These settings can significantly impact your trading performance and costs. Only enable features you fully understand and can afford to risk.
</Warning>

## Farmer Mode

### What is Farmer Mode?

<Card icon="seedling" title="Automated Token Farming">
**Farmer mode automatically launches tokens** at regular intervals based on your configuration, creating a continuous stream of new positions for potential profit farming.
</Card>

### Farmer Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Farmer">
    **Toggle farmer mode on/off**: When enabled, OneClick will automatically launch tokens according to your schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Delay">
    **Configure launch intervals**:
    - Minimum: 30 seconds (for testing)
    - Recommended: 5-30 minutes (sustainable farming)
    - Maximum: 24 hours (long-term strategy)
    - Dynamic: Adjust based on market conditions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Safety Limits">
    **Set protective boundaries**:
    - Maximum launches per hour/day
    - Total daily spending caps
    - Consecutive failure limits
    - Balance threshold requirements
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Batch Deployment

### Batch Configuration

<Card icon="layer-group" title="Multi-Token Deployment">
**Deploy multiple tokens simultaneously** for diversification and efficiency:
- **Batch count**: 2-10 tokens per batch
- **Staggered timing**: Spread deployments over time
- **Resource management**: Distribute SOL across tokens
- **Coordination**: Ensure successful batch completion
</Card>

### Batch Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Batch Deploy">
    **Toggle batch deployment**: Activate multi-token launching capability
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Batch Count">
    **Choose number of tokens**:
    - Small batch: 2-3 tokens (conservative)
    - Medium batch: 4-6 tokens (balanced)
    - Large batch: 7-10 tokens (aggressive)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Timing">
    **Set deployment intervals**:
    - Simultaneous: All tokens at once
    - Staggered: Spread over minutes/hours
    - Random: Randomized deployment timing
  </Step>
</Steps>
