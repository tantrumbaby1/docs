## OneClick Configuration

### Enable OneClick

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activation">
    **Toggle OneClick enabled** to activate instant launching from any text selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="SOL Amount">
    **Set default SOL amount** for OneClick purchases:
    - Minimum: 0.001 SOL (for testing)
    - Recommended: 0.01-0.1 SOL (for active trading)
    - Maximum: 10 SOL (for high-conviction plays)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    **Choose confirmation level**:
    - No confirmation (instant)
    - Quick confirmation (3-second countdown)
    - Full confirmation (review before launch)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### OneClick Safety

<Warning>
**Speed vs Safety**: OneClick prioritizes speed over safety checks. Only enable with funds you can afford to lose quickly.
</Warning>
